ᗧ··· ABOUT THE FEST

Daksha Yanthra is the annual two-day technical and cultural extravaganza of College of Engineering Attingal, Kerala. This year, we're teleporting to the golden age of arcade gaming!

Step into a world of pixel-perfect nostalgia — from classic Pac-Man mazes to retro boss fights — where creativity, technology, and 8-bit magic collide. Whether you're a code warrior, a stage performer, or a gaming legend, there's a level here for everyone.
